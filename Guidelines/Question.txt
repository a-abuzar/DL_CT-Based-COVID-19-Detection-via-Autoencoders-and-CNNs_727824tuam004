COVID-19 Detection from CT Scans Using Autoencoder-Based Anomaly Detection and CNN Classification

Manual screening of CT scans for COVID-19 is slow and requires expert radiologists, who may be scarce during outbreaks. This project proposes a deep learning system that combines autoencoder-based anomaly detection with CNN classification to automatically identify COVID-19 patterns in CT scans, enabling rapid and reliable screening.
